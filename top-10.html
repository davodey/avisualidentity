10 Common Website Mistakes You Should Know About

Choosing who builds your website is one of the most important decisions you will make. Your website is vital to your success and is often the first interaction prospective clients and customers will have with you. So its really important you make a good first impression, and provide them the info they are looking for. Lets go over the top 10 Mistakes that local business owners make with their website and see how this can harm your reputation.

1.Your site looks uncredibile.

Have you been to a website, and thought to yourself "I would never enter my credit card information on this site." We all have. The look and feel of a website has great importance. Dont let a non-professional design your site. Having your neighbor, family friend, or even your son or daughter design your website, may sound like a good idea. In reality it can harm the credibility of your company.

2. Your website is not responsive.

How does your site look on a mobile phone? It should look beautiful! Don't give mobile viewers a bad experience. Sites we build are all responsive ready; you don't even have the option to not go responsive. It’s that important.

3. Using services like Wix.

Are you using an outdated, non-scalable Wix template? Wix is known, to provide a cheap template design service. Regardless of what they say, buyer beware. The templates are not SEO friendly, poorly designed, and not customizable.

4. No CMS.

What’s a CMS? It’s a “content management system.” It empowers the owner of the website to easily manage, maintain, and update content on their own, without the help of designer. We use custom Wordpress CMS builds for our clients. We find its the easiest system out there for our clients to manage.

5. Non-semantic markup.

If your site was not coded by a professional its most likely non-semantic and structured poorly. Semantic markup not only helps with SEO, but also makes it easy for other designers or developers to work on your site if you ever need help. Designers and Developers often turn away jobs, or cant help you, if the code is not written correctly. We hand code all our websites using proper HTML5 & CSS3 semantics.

6. Not designed on UCD principles.

What’s UCD? It stands for user-centered design. It is a set of techniques that professional web designers use, to see how your users will interact with your website. All our designs go through lengthy UCD process, that involves interviewing our clients, researching, testing, and revising.

7. No user testing.

How do you know if your site is going to perform unless it’s tested with users that model your end user? We will do that for you. All designs come with FREE UX testing to ensure the best results.

8. Not designing for business goals and objectives.

Design is not art, the process of building a website is long if you want it to meets your companies goals and objectives. Its more than just colors and layout, its about user experience.

9. Choosing proper plugins.

If your familiar with plugins you know that not all plugins are good. Some can damage your site. So before you download that plugin do your research. Before we start designing your site, we will research what’s out there and choose only the best up to date plugins to use, that you can guarantee will never have a problem. If you dont know what a plugin is, dont worry, well explain it to you during the process.

10. Not having a website at all.

It is nearly impossible for your business to perform up to its potential without a website. That is why we offer all clients the lowest prices in the valley. We want you to have that site you need at a low cost. Each project is priced independently but our research shows that we come in 80% lower than most agencies or companies!
